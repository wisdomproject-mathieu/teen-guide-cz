## Free vs Paid Split: 20% / 80%

### Always Free (never gate):
- SOS + emergency contacts + Linka bezpečí 116 111
- Mood check-in (Cítím)
- 3 core speeches (1 per mood, best ones)
- 1 breathing exercise (box)
- 7-day diary history
- Basic warrior card share
- Warrior rank display (view only)

### Premium:
- Full speech library (17+ speeches × 3 intensities)
- AI chat (Opičák) — per-use cost, MUST be gated
- Full mood calendar + unlimited diary
- Warrior rank progression + unlocks
- Smart notifications
- Premium warrior share cards
- Video shorts (deferred — needs HeyGen)

## Pricing (Czech-localized)
- Free: 0 CZK / forever
- Monthly (Opičí bojovník): 99 CZK/měs (~€4)
- Annual (Opičí válečník): 799 CZK/rok (~€2.7/měs) — DEFAULT, featured
- **Never price above 999 CZK/year** for teen segment
- 7-day free trial mandatory entry (no credit card)

## 4 Revenue Pillars
1. B2C Subscription (66%) — target 500 paying users by M6, 2000 by M12
2. School/Institutional B2B (20%) — 15-25 CZK/student/month, target 3 schools by M9
3. One-time warrior packs (9%) — 149 CZK speech bundles
4. Wellness brand sponsorships (5%) — non-intrusive, manually selected Czech brands

## Non-negotiable Rules
- NEVER gate safety (SOS, crisis, emergency contacts)
- No ads on teen mental health experience. EVER.
- Don't start collecting subscriptions before product earns it
- Localize pricing — never use US/EU baseline for Czech teens

## Phased Rollout
- Phase 0 (Now): Build value, ship voice + AI + cold start. Don't monetize yet.
- Phase 1 (M1-2): Soft launch paid tier with 7-day trial via Stripe
- Phase 2 (M3-4): School licensing pilot (3 Prague schools)
- Phase 3 (M5-6): Warrior packs + first sponsorship
- Phase 4 (M8+): Family plan (1,299 CZK/yr), health insurance partnerships

## Target Funnel
100% discover → 60% open → 40% first speech → 25% return 3d → 15% trial → 7% paid

## Unit Economics Y1
- 2,000 MAU → 140 paying → ~11,000 CZK/mo subs + ~12,000 B2B = ~25,000 CZK/mo
- Costs: ~8,000 CZK/mo (AI + TTS + hosting)
