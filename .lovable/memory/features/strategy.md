Key features from strategy (prioritized):
1. Emotional intensity slider (1-5) on mood check-in → routes to intensity-matched TTS settings
2. Warrior rank system: Opičí nováček (0) → Opičí bojovník (100) → Opičí válečník (300) → Opičí legenda (750)
3. Anonymous peer echo: "X opičích válečníků cítilo tohle dnes" after mood log
4. Share-to-TikTok warrior card: canvas-based 15s clip with quote, rank, mood transformation
5. Smart micro-interventions: Sunday 9pm, Monday 7am, post-school, exam-aware
6. 90-second cold start: mood → value in under 90s
7. Video shorts (needs HeyGen — deferred)
8. Discord bot (external — deferred)

Research insights:
- Gamification does NOT improve retention; reminders + perceived human contact do
- 75% Gen Z quit if not personalized
- Czech teen mental health market is uncontested
- Ranks > streaks for identity-based retention
